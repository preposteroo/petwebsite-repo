About Us

                FurEver Home is dedicated to using technology to help
            cats and dogs find their homes. We hope to increase 
            public awareness of the local pets who are just waiting 
            to be adopted. Whether you're already a pet parent 
            looking to expand your family or adopting for the first
            time, FurEver Home makes adopting accessible to everyone. 
            From the comfort of your own computer, you can search for
            the pet that matches you best. 
            
                Have a pet who needs a new home? Fill out our simple form
            and have it posted to our website. Need info on pet care?
            Check out our pages on Dog and Cat Care. We're here to help,
            don't hesitate to contact us!
            
                There are so many cats and dogs that are out of this world
